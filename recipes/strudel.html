Apple strudel

Description

Here is a recipe for an apple strudel

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Puff pastry/dough
  • Crystal sugar
  • Raisins
  • Apples
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Stir the apples, sugar, raisins, and flour in a bowl.
  2. Place each puff pastry on a lightly floured work surface and roll into two rectangles.
  3. Fill and seal each pastry, then transfer to a baking sheet.
  4. Brush on the egg wash and cut slits into the top.
  5. Bake until golden brown.
