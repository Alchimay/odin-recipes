Simple chicken wrap

Description

Here is a recipe for a very simple chicken wrap

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Bell pepper
  • Lettuce
  • Chicken

Steps

  1. Place tortilla on a plate
  2. Dice the tomatoes, cucumber and bell pepper
  3. Take the mayonnaise and spread it across the middle of the tortilla from top to bottom
  4. Take the diced mix from step 2 and spread it on top of the mayonnaise on the tortilla
  5. Add few pieces of chicken
  6. Add few lettuce leaves on top
  7. Fold the bottom of the tortilla and wrap the sides up to make a cylinder
